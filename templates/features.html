Features

Enhanced Professions

Craftsmen

Master artisans with expanded production chains, new buildings, and specialized workshops. Create masterpiece items for nobles and earn prestigious commissions.

Scholars

Delve into the mysteries of science and medicine with new research opportunities, university positions, and the ability to establish medical practices and schools.

Patrons

Expand your mercantile empire with new trading options, banking services, and the ability to finance town projects for increased influence and profit.

Rogues

Master the shadows with enhanced thieving abilities, smuggling operations, and the new Mercenary Captain specialization that allows you to hire fighters for protection or aggression.

Political System

The Reforged Mod completely overhauls the political system of The Guild 2: Renaissance, making town politics more dynamic and meaningful.

Enhanced Office Benefits

Each political office provides unique abilities and privileges that can dramatically alter your gameplay strategy.

Improved Court System

A completely revamped court system with new charges, defenses, and consequences that make legal battles more strategic.

Town Development

Influence the growth and prosperity of towns through policies, taxation, and public works that affect all citizens.

Factional Politics

Join or create political factions that compete for control of the town council, offering new ways to gain allies and influence.

Dynasty Management

Enhanced Family System

Expanded marriage options, child-rearing mechanics, and family relationships that affect your dynasty's future.

Education & Training

New options for educating your children and heirs, with different schools and mentors providing various benefits.

Legacy System

Build your family's reputation through generations, unlocking special abilities and opportunities based on your dynasty's history.

Economic Improvements

  • Dynamic market system with fluctuating prices based on supply and demand
  • Expanded production chains and business opportunities
  • Trade routes between towns with varying profitability
  • Banking system with loans, investments, and interest
  • Form business partnerships and guilds with other players or AI dynasties

Combat & Security

  • Improved combat system with new tactics and formations
  • Hire guards and security for your businesses and family
  • Expanded duel and combat options for resolving conflicts
  • Mercenary system for hiring fighters for protection or aggression
  • Fortify your properties against attacks and robberies
