About The Guild 2: Renaissance

The Guild 2: Renaissance is a medieval life simulation and economic strategy game that immerses players in the dynamic world of medieval Europe. As the head of a dynasty, players navigate the complex social, political, and economic landscape of the time.

Released in 2010 as an expansion to The Guild 2, Renaissance enhances the original game with improved AI, new buildings, professions, and scenarios, creating a more immersive medieval experience.

Gameplay

In The Guild 2: Renaissance, players select a character class based on professions common to the period—patrons, craftsmen, scholars, or rogues—each with unique buildings, abilities, and play styles. The game unfolds across multiple generations as you build your dynasty's wealth, power, and influence.

Key features of the game include:

  • Operating businesses to generate income
  • Participating in town politics
  • Competing with rival dynasties
  • Managing family relationships and marriages
  • Engaging in legal (or illegal) activities to further your goals

The Reforged Mod

While Renaissance improved upon its predecessors, the game still had limitations and bugs. This is where the Reforged Mod comes in, created by dedicated fans to address these issues and further enhance the gameplay experience.

The Reforged Mod is a community-driven project that aims to fix bugs, balance gameplay, add new content, and improve the overall quality of The Guild 2: Renaissance. Learn more about the specific enhancements on our Reforged Mod page.

Our Mission

Our mission is to preserve and enhance The Guild 2: Renaissance through the Reforged Mod, ensuring this unique medieval simulation continues to be enjoyed by players for years to come. We aim to create the definitive version of the game that realizes its full potential.
