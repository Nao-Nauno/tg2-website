The Reforged Mod

The Reforged Mod is a comprehensive enhancement for The Guild 2: Renaissance, created by passionate members of the game's community. Our goal is to fix bugs, improve gameplay mechanics, add new content, and transform Renaissance into the definitive Guild experience.

Key Improvements

Bug Fixes

  • Fixed crash issues during long play sessions
  • Resolved AI pathing problems
  • Fixed broken quest chains and events
  • Corrected item and building functionality
  • Addressed multiplayer desync issues

Balance Changes

  • Rebalanced profession income and progression
  • Adjusted building costs and maintenance
  • Improved AI competition behavior
  • Balanced political offices and benefits
  • Refined court and justice system

New Content

  • Added new professions and career paths
  • Introduced additional buildings and upgrades
  • Created new items and artifacts
  • Expanded quest lines and events
  • Added new maps and scenarios

Quality of Life

  • Improved UI and information display
  • Enhanced camera controls
  • Added keyboard shortcuts
  • Refined production and employee management
  • Streamlined diplomatic and political actions

Compatibility

The Reforged Mod is designed to work with the Steam version of The Guild 2: Renaissance. It is not compatible with earlier versions of The Guild 2 or with other expansions unless specified.

Installation is simple with our automatic installer, which backs up your original game files and applies all necessary modifications. Detailed installation instructions are available on the Download page.
